Setup (Pre-Workshop)

It is vitally important that your R and Rstudio installations are running before we commence. Instructions for performing this are available here. Please ensure you have everything installed and running correctly BEFORE Tuesday 19th September

Failure to have a working installation prior to the workshop will not only limit the amount you can learn, but will also limit what others can learn from the session, as we lose time trying to fix your machine. Importantly, installing R on a managed device can be unpredictably difficult or easy. Please assume you have a difficult device and prepare early.

If you require assistance, we are more than happy to help resolve any issues. Staff are available to help you with your installation between 2-5pm, Monday 18th September at Telethon Kids Institute, 108 North Tce, Adelaide. Otherwise, please email for help

If you are unable to get an installation working on your own laptop, please sign-up for a free account at Posit Cloud
